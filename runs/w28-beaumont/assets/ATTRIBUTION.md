## Note on subject photos
No photo of the three Beaumont children (or the reported "tall blond man") is used in this render.
No free-licensed image of them exists on Wikimedia Commons (checked this run — zero results), and even if
one surfaced, KNOWN_BAD policy + the sensitivity of real, identifiable missing children in a still-open
case argues against using it regardless of license. All three assets above are real, license-clear
period/location photography that establishes setting without depicting the children or any named suspect.
