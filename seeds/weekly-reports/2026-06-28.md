1. **Shorts-feed reach is gated by swipe-through; standalone ANSWER Shorts die.** The 9× spread between best (320v / 60.3%) and worst (34v / 12.7%) is entirely retention. ANSWER/payoff clips have no self-contained hook and cliff in the first 3s.
2. **Cold-start long-form suppression is temporary, not permanent.** Tunguska went from "0v / suppressed" to 112v via Browse+Suggested with no external seed — overturns KNOWN_BAD.
3. **The audience is 100% male and ~80% over 65.** Older demo = low Shorts engagement (0 likes is normal here) but strong long-form AVD (7:13). Lean into long-form; script for clarity, not Gen-Z pacing.
