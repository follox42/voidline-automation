## 4. Learnings distilled (appended to LEARNINGS.md)

1. **Suppression has a sharp ~24h cutoff and dominates content quality.** The v3
   trio is the proof: answer (06-07)=106, hook (06-08, S-tier)=0, twist (06-10)=26.
   When a suppression window opens, *everything* published into it is clamped —
   you cannot rescue it with better creative. **Halt uploads, don't out-create.**
2. **~300v Shorts ceiling confirmed a 3rd time** (v2_twist 297, v1_twist 279,
   prior v1_twist 274 — all flat, none ever crossed ~300). The comment-velocity
   lever (cutter v2 outro) that's meant to break it is **still untested**, because
   every cutter-v2 Short (v3 batch) was suppressed before it could run.
3. **ANSWER-position framing travels standalone** (candidate, 2/3 data points):
   v3_answer (106) and v1_answer (84) each beat their batch's HOOK short. The
   hook→twist→answer dogma may undervalue solution-framed hooks.
